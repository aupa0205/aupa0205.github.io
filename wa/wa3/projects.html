My Projects

The Aero-Buff

For my Design Foundations class, we were tasked with creating a potential monument for CU's campus. My group went with a statue of a Buffalo that had rocket wings and a pilot's helmet to give recognition to the aerospace engineering department and put a statue in an area that doesn't have a lot.

The Arachnoball

Also in my Design Foundations class, myself and a couple of other students were told to create a new toy that might be able to be sold in stores. We created a cat toy called the Arachnoball that was simply a ball that had spider legs on it for cats to be able to chase.
